About Us

Little Kitchen is owned and run by Hannah Baker, in a quaint little spot in Fremantle Australia. We are passionate about making good food, and want to create a place where people can come to relax, and enjoy themselves. Our cafe, Little Kitchen, is an integral part of the Fremantle scene. You can hire out the café as a venue for weddings and other events by simply clicking the 'Book Us' button in our menu. Our cafe has a unique atmosphere, simple but beautiful décor and delicious food!

Zero Waste Policy

As Second Slice Cafe has become a popular spot for coffee and small bites by the Northcote community, we feel that we should do our part to help the environment. We have a zero waste policy and use only organic locally sourced ingredients in all our dishes. All our customers share a passion for sustainability and reducing waste.

    Assuring no single use plastics are used (such as take away containers)

    Recycling and using a composting systems

    Using our own garden, with fresh ingredients being used in dishes each day

    Encouraging customers to bring in reusable coffee cups

Eat with Us

What better way to start your day with a carefully crafted, hand poured cup of coffee. All coffee beans are sourced directly from a farmer, ensuring a sustainable and financially stable model for the producers to rely on. Our coffee is a single origin bean ensuring consistent quality, completeness of flavour and a seamless approach to understanding varietals and styles. It is lightly roasted which captures the essence of chocolate and caramel. Enjoy our coffee black, white or filtered.

Contact Us

We’re open all day, every day from seven to three! Our kitchen can meet gluten free, vegan and other dietary requirements. We offer split billing and there are no additional fees or charges, even on public holidays! Secure parking is available on collie street around the corner from us.
